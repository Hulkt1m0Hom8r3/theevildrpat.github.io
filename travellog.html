Travel Log

Day 1

So far you guys have started a building on fire which killed several people, actively killed a soon-to-be grandfather gnome, looted the bar you started on fire, looted both the living and dead gnome (also tried to dismember and keep a cooked dead gnome), and tried to break in to the residence above the bar.

Day 2

The group visits Totally Tonics and picks up some supplies. Randy tries to revisit the witch, but with no luck. The group visits the candy shop only to realize it is now a weapons shop. We find a group of monster elvish children locked in the basement who were given nightglow for bathing purposes, slayed the children then the store manager (store owned by Don Crustbluff). We then go to a river to wash up and to the armor store, where nobody is hurt or killed. We venture out into the woods and find the man-eating bear after luring him with some intentionally burnt wolf steaks. Bear is easily slain, and the druid darts off into the woods. Party pockets bear balls.

Day 3

Somehow 9 months have passed and everyone wakes to a burned up ash pile and long beards. Green Na’vi and Small Grey dude joins the group. Hector checks his stomach to see if he is still pregnant but his child is gone. There is a “C” mark on his stomach. Then the group encounters a farmer who offers them information in exchange for passing three riddles. The group succeeds with flying colors and the farmer hands over 3 pieces of parchment. The group decides to kill him anyways but they fail and instead shit their pants, and cover the parchment in shit in the process. Then we covered it in more shit. We read two notes, then proceeded north to the mountains (leaving behind a note for Dogmeat) when we came across a barn with oinking coming from inside, it’s pig fuckers.We battle into the night….
